<p> <i>Arru</i> is an app to help you to track and analyze your expenses.</p> <p> <br> <b>Features:</b> </p> <ul> <li>Light/Dark mode</li> <li>Wide screen support</li> <li>Local backups</li> <li>Data export</li> <li>Transaction baskets tracking your total expenditure with optional product, category, shop and producer spending tracking</li> <li>Comparisons between prices at different shops</li> <li>Ranking of categories and shops based on total money spent</li> <li>Merging capabilities for categories, shops, products and producers</li> </ul>
